KnowHows
Available commands:
/knowhows to -- performs a natural language search
/knowhows search -- performs search for explicit request
/knowhows track -- tracks and indexes one of your repositories
/knowhows untrack -- untracks and unindexes one of your repositories
/knowhows help -- shows this help message

KnowHows is a Slack app that allows users to search repositories for people who have knowledge on certain parts of code.

Track
Add the commit histories for your repositories on GitHub
Locate
Find APIs associated with a software problem
Rank
Discover where the experience is on your team
Developed by Robbie Uvanni, Ben Leach, and Alex Aplin under the guidance of Kostadin Damevski for the VCU Capstone Design Expo 2018.
